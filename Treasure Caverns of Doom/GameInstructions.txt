Welcome to Treasure Caverns of Doom ! 

In this action adventure game, you've been assigned as the hero by a desperate old man who begs you to retrieve his artefacts lurking deep inside a network of caves filled with both monsters and rewards. You must successfully retrieve 5 artefacts from the network of caves and escape the caverns in order to accomplish your mission successfully. Otherwise you will fail if the monsters turn you into a corpse or your oil inside your lantern runs out ! 

As you progress deeper in the cave, the challenges grow stronger but so should you !

Best of luck, adventurer !
